Help with identity documents

Primary identity documents

Examples inlcude:

  • current passport
  • driving licence
  • biometric residence permit
  • certificate of naturalisation as a British citizen
  • permanent residence card

Secondary identity documents

Examples of proof of date of birth inlcude:

  • birth certificate
  • marriage certificate
  • NHS medical card

Examples of proof of address inlcude a recent:

  • utility bill
  • bank statement
  • Council Tax bill

Additional forms

Based on the claimants application answers you may also need to ask them to complete these forms during their initial work search interview.

  • ES567SJP - Attending a training or education course
  • ST1 - Student or ex student
  • A15/B7 - part time working setup electronic form
