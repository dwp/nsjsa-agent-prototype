WFM-1B

Iteration 2.4

Problem statement

When MWAC starts to scale to contact centres not all jobcentres will be using MWAC for NSJSA appointments.

How can we ensure contact centre agents know which jobcentres require appointments to be booked in MWAC or LMS?

Hypothesis

By ensuring that contact agents are informed about MWAC roll out we will make sure that appointments for NSJSA are being booked in the appropriate system.

This will enable a smooth transition from the old LMS system to the new MWAC service.
