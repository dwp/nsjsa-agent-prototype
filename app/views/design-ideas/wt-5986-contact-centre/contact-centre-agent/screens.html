Contact centre - update Nino

Redesign of the update Nino journey for new claim checks in the Contact centre.

Problem statement

The Update Nino journey for Contact centre agents currently displays the whole of the customer statement. This is against GDPR principles of only displaying relevant information to allow the agent to conduct the activity.

Operational instructions indicates the following fields are required to be checked on a call with the claimant:

  • Full name
  • National Insurance number
  • Date of birth
  • the first line of address and postcode

The Update Nino journey needs to be re-designed to ensure compliance with GDPR principles by only displaying the minimum amount of information required by CCAâ€™s to complete the mismatch activity.
