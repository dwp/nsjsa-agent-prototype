Workflow Management 1a: Iteration 2

Hypothesis 1: Claim status

We believe that introducing the new claim status 'processing started' when an agent selects 'Get a new claim' to allocate the claim to that agent, will reduce the risk of claim loss, reduce the risk of more than one agent working on the same new claim, and will avoid cherry picking.

We will know this wehen we test a prototype and users tell us:

  • The status change from 'Success' to 'Processing started' is clear
  • They cannot cherry pick claims
  • They would find the new claim again, if their session times out, or they navigate away from the claim
Hypothesis 2: Claims list with filter

We believe that showing agents a filterable list of all claims they have updated last, will reduce the risk of claims getting lost and saves time finding claims.

We will know this wehen we test a prototype and users tell us:

  • The list acts as a safeguard against human error and technical failure
  • The filter functionality makes it easy to find claims
