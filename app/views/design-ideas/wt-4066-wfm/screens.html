Version 2: Workflow Management 1a

Problem statement

The current process to move a claim from the Contact Centre to the Service Centre involves many teams and several manual actions. These manual actions are time consuming and are at risk of user error and claim loss. They also increase the claim decision waiting time. Agents send sensitive claimant data by email and store it in SharePoint folders. This is a potential data security risk. The business does not have a full view of claim volumes at different stages of the process. This means it is challenging to identify where to prioritise resource.
