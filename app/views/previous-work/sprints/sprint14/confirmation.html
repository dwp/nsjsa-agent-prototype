Identity details received

If you've provided a mobile number or email address, we will send you confirmation of your application and keep you updated on its progress.

What happens next?

We'll process your application.

If you are eligible

If you can get New Style JSA, we will let you know and set up a time to agree what you must do to look for work. We will discuss an agreement called a ‘Claimant Commitment’. We will contact you within the next 10 working days.

The appointment will be with a work coach and will normally be over the phone.

You must speak to the work coach and agree your Claimant Commitment before we can pay you.

If you are not eligible

If you cannot get New Style JSA, we will send you a letter within 10 working days to explain why.
