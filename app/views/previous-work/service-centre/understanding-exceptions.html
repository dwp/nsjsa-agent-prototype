Understanding exceptions to automation

The goal of the system is to automate claims through to an appointment being needed to be booked.

Automation isn't always possible though. We have to give any claims that can't be automated to an agent to be manually processed.

These claims are called exceptions and they will drop into a bucket depending on the type of claim and what needs to happen to it.
