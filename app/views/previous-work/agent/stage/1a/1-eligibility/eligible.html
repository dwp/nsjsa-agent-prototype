Based on your answers, you may be eligible for New Style Jobseeker’s Allowance

To get it, you’ll need to apply and confirm your identity.

To apply, you’ll need:

  • your National Insurance number
  • your bank or building society account details (or those of a family member or trusted friend)
  • any employment details for the past 6 months, including employer contact details and dates you worked with them
  • any private pension or annuity statement letter if you get payments from one

To confirm your identity you’ll need:

  • a mobile phone number
  • an email address

You will also need 2 of the following:

  • P60 or payslips
  • a valid UK passport
  • tax credit information
